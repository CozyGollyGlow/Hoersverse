Zambuko (astronaut)

From Kwikepedia, the free encyclopedia

Zambuko (born April 23, 2012) is an EASA biologist and astronaut.

Background

Zambuko was born on April 23, 2012, in Zebrica. Fascinated by the natural sciences from a young age, Zambuko pursued their studies at the University of Kwadube, graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Biology with a focus on extraterrestrial ecosystems. After obtaining their degree in 2038, they joined the Zebrican Biological Research Institute, focusing on cellular adaptation under varying gravitational conditions.

Stardust 21

In December 2038, Zambuko was selected to join EASA Astronaut Group 42, following a collaborative program between EASA and Kwadube's Space Agency. Known for their expertise in biological adaptation, they were chosen as a key scientific member of the Stardust 21 mission, tasked with studying microbial resilience in deep space conditions.

Personnal life

Aurora has maintained a lifelong friendship with fellow astronaut Concorde, whom she met during childhood. Outside her engineering career, Aurora is an avid competitive video gamer, known for her exceptional skills in multiplayer strategy games. She is deeply private and avoids photographs whenever possible, preferring to let her technical expertise speak for itself.
