Wonderbolts

From Kwikepedia, the free encyclopedia

The Wonderbolts are an equestrian elite squad of Pegasus ponies who perform aerial acrobatics and demonstrations.

Background

Hierarchy

Notable Wonderbolt Captains

  • Honour Guard - 1942 to 1950 - War hero.
  • Split Second - 1966 to 1972 - Athleticism medallist.
  • Haute École - 1979 to 1988 - Fashion enthusiast.
  • Pony Hawk - 1988 to 1993 - Famous skate athlete.
  • High Jump - 1993 to 2002 - Regarded as one of the most famous Wonderbolt captains. Known for having introduced the iconic Wonderbolt uniform still used to this day.
  • Nine Yards - 2002 to 2008 - Football athlete.
  • Spitfire - 2008 to 2014 - Elite flyer.
