Concorde (astronaut)

From Kwikepedia, the free encyclopedia For other uses, see Concorde (disambiguation)

Concorde (born December 17, 2017) is an EASA astronaut.

Background

Concorde was born December 17, 2017. Their parents are Supermarine Spitfire, captain of the Wonderbolts from 2008 to 2014, and Soarin, captain of the Wonderbolts from 2014 to 2032. They came out as non-binary at the age of 14, they graduated from Cloudsdale Grammer School in 2034, and received a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Sciences with a specialization in aeronautics from Canterlot University in 2038.

EASA

On December 6, 2038, they were revelead to be one of 26 candidates selected to EASA Astronaut Group 42, to report for training in January 2039. They were one of the 7 candidates from the Canterlot University's partner program with EASA.

Personnal life

Concorde is fluid in multiple languages including Equestrian, Kirean and Gryphish.
